For both alpine and nordic skiing, weather conditions are very important

a fine "powder" of snow is ideal for maximum controll

Temperature is also an important variable. If its too cold and you will be miserable

but if its too warm, the snow will melt and become "slushy"

Temperature Preciptation Overall Quality
Below 20 Fahrenheit Clear skies Decent Quality - Cold
20 - 30 Fahrenheit Light snow Great Quality
Above 30 Fahrenheit Heavy snow / Sleet Decent Quality - Snow will get slushy
